Information to including when asking help or when reporting problems

When you ask questions on the mailing list, or when you report a problem (either on the mailing list or via the bug tracker, you can help us out by giving as much hard information as you can.

Tell us which version of our software you're using :

  • installed from source (which version? a specific distribution, or from SVN)
  • installed from a binary distribution ? Which one ?

But also whether you're using arm-wince-mingw32ce or arm-wince-cegcc tools.

Which platform are you using to develop ? (Linux, Cygwin, ..)

What is the nature of your problem ?

Can you send us a small source that demonstrates the problem ?
